Building stronger relationships between Thailand and Japan

Since receiving authorisation from the Thai Ministry of Labour in 2018, Hida Hikaru Recruitment has supported the recruitment and preparation of Thai professionals who are ready to work in Japan.

We work closely with candidates and companies to help create reliable, responsible, and sustainable employment relationships.

International Recruitment
